How it works

Six steps.
No copy-paste.

1
Open a folder
Click "Open Folder" — SlideHTML sets up your project and generates CLAUDE.md automatically.
2
Talk to your AI
Run Claude Code or Gemini CLI and ask for a slide. The AI already knows the rules.
3
Slides appear live
SlideHTML watches the folder. Files are detected the instant they're written.
4
Full Chromium rendering
CDN libraries, animations, charts, video embeds — everything works. It's a real browser.
5
Edit and iterate
See your slides in the editor. Ask the AI to fix something. Changes reflect instantly.
6
Present
Hit Present. Full screen. Arrow keys to navigate. ESC to exit. That's it.
